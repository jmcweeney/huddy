# Huddy — Let's Get Real for a Sec

Before we build anything, we need to know where things actually stand. No judgment, no wrong answers. Just want to make sure what we create actually moves the needle — for the brand and for the dogs.

Grab a coffee. This should take 20 minutes, tops.

---

## The Business

- How many orders are you doing a month right now, roughly?
- What's splitting one-time buyers vs. subscribers?
- What's your churn looking like on subscriptions — people staying or dropping off?
- Which product is flying and which is just sitting there?
- Do you have a sense of what a customer is worth to you over time?

---

## The Site & Platform

- Why Lightspeed — was that intentional or just how it started?
- What's working on the site and what drives you crazy about it?
- Are you tracking anything — visitors, bounce rate, conversions?
- Where's your traffic actually coming from?
- Have you run paid ads? What happened?
- Are you running the site yourself or do you have help?

---

## The Customer

- Who's your best customer — like if you had to describe one person?
- Have you ever just talked to someone after they bought? What do they tell you?
- Where are your buyers mostly located?
- What's the primary lens they come in with — rescue advocate, eco-conscious, dog lover?
- Anyone buying in bulk yet — parks, vets, rescues?

---

## The Mission

- When you tell people about the dog meat trade angle, what's the typical reaction?
- Has anyone covered you — press, podcasts, blogs?
- Which of your three rescue partners has the most active community?
- Have any of them pushed Huddy to their audience yet? What happened?
- What's the one dog story that makes you remember exactly why you started this?

---

## Social & Word of Mouth

- Where are you most active and what feels natural vs. forced?
- The trash can ads — who came up with those and did they move anything measurable?
- Are customers posting about Huddy on their own? Where?
- Do you have an email list and are you actually using it?

---

## The Real Talk

- Three years from now, Huddy is crushing it — what does that actually look like to you?
- What's the honest thing holding growth back right now?
- Any hesitation about moving platforms or rebuilding the site?
- How hands-on do you want to stay as this grows?
- What's the budget reality for the next six months?
- Who else is in your corner on this — advisor, partner, investor?

---

## Last One

**If someone lands on your site today and leaves without buying — what do you think stopped them?**

That's the one. Answer that honestly and we'll know exactly where to start.
